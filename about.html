    About Us

    Ami de Farmer is recognized the world over for delivering a unique system that offers world-class refinement while remaining deeply rooted in its Indian heritage. For more than a century, Ami de Farmer has brought together the unique beauty and traditions from across India in an experience that highlights true Indian hospitality. This draws on the time-honored traditions central to the Indian homecoming, from the refreshing simplicity of the welcome drink to the careful choreography of the dinner service. The Sea Shore Inn has helped to put many Indian destinations on the map. From the resorts of Goa to the upcoming business hubs of India’s Silicon Valley, an iconic Sea Shore Inn is a mainstay of the destination. By providing jobs to the local community, supporting endangered artisan practices and reviving regal history at palaces where royal families are still in residence, The Sea Shore Inn offers a rare insider’s point of view to the fascinating story of India – from Maharaja to modern day. With 98 hotels in 62 locations across India, Ami de Farmer offers guests the refined luxury of heritage combined with effortlessly efficient service and local expertise to create a moment to remember. In India, Ami de Farmer chain of hotels are strategically located to offer the class experience in business hubs, industrial towns and metropolises, as well as the most remote pilgrimage centers, historic and natural destinations. The group has extended its international presence with 15 hotels in the Maldives, Malaysia, the UK, USA, Bhutan, Sri Lanka, Africa and the Middle East.
